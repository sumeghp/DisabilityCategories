Learning Disabilities

Examples: Dyslexia, Dyscalculia, Dysgraphia

Characteristics: Difficulties in reading, writing, math, or information processing

Sensory Impairments

Examples: Visual impairment, Hearing impairment

Characteristics: Reduced or lack of sensory input affecting learning

Physical Disabilities

Examples: Cerebral Palsy, Muscular Dystrophy

Characteristics: Limitations in mobility or physical functioning

Intellectual Disabilities

Characteristics: Limitations in intellectual functioning and adaptive behavior

Autism Spectrum Disorders (ASD)

Characteristics: Challenges with social skills, repetitive behaviors, speech and nonverbal communication

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

Characteristics: Difficulty paying attention, controlling impulsive behaviors, or being overly active
